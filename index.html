100 Days of Prototyping

Inspired by various 100 days projects, I decided to start my own 100 days journey of prototyping. Prototyping is a powerful tool for designers to bring ideas from the head into the world. The following prototyping ideas come from my daily work, design communities, random thoughts, friends' suggestions, etc. I inended to prototype with web technology, it is still an ongoing effort, and I enjoyed learning new bits every day.
